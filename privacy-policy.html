Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024 · Effective immediately

Short version: We don't collect your name, email, or personal details. The app works fully offline. The only data involved is your Advertising ID (used by Google AdMob to show ads) and purchase records (handled by RevenueCat). Your bookmarks, settings, and notes progress are stored only on your device and never sent to us.

1. Who We Are

O-Level Prep is developed by Rajat Galar (TechnoWizard). If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at itechnowizard12@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Your location
  • Your contacts, messages, or files
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Any personally identifiable information

The app has no user login system and no server-side database of user data.

2.2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following is stored on your device only and is never transmitted to us:

  • Bookmarks — chapters you star, stored in a local Room database
  • App preferences — theme, font size, language, notification time, stored in DataStore
  • Quiz progress — session-only, cleared when you leave the quiz
  • Score card images — temporarily stored in cache when you share a result, deleted automatically

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data permanently.

2.3 Advertising ID (via Google AdMob)

The app shows advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may access your device's Advertising ID (a resettable identifier) to show relevant ads. We do not directly access or store this identifier — it is used entirely by Google's advertising system.

You can reset or opt out of personalised ads at any time in your Android device settings under Privacy → Ads.

2.4 Purchase Data (via RevenueCat)

If you purchase the PRO upgrade, your transaction is processed by Google Play. We use RevenueCat to verify purchase status. RevenueCat receives your Google Play purchase token to confirm your entitlement. We do not see or store your payment card details.

3. Third-Party Services

The following third-party services are integrated and have their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob Used to serve advertisements. May collect device identifiers and usage signals to show relevant ads. Google Privacy Policy →
RevenueCat Used to manage and verify in-app purchases and subscriptions. Receives purchase tokens from Google Play. RevenueCat Privacy Policy →
Google Play Billing All payments are processed by Google Play. We do not handle payment information directly. Google Privacy Policy →

4. How We Use Information

The limited data involved is used only to:

  • Show advertisements to support the free version of the app (via AdMob)
  • Verify your PRO purchase status so the correct features are unlocked (via RevenueCat)
  • Save your preferences locally so the app works the way you configured it

We do not use any data for analytics, profiling, or selling to third parties.

5. Advertising and Your Choices

The free version of the app shows ads. These ads help us keep the app free for all students.

  • Opt out of personalised ads: Go to Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • Reset Advertising ID: Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset Advertising ID
  • Remove ads entirely: Purchase the PRO upgrade — PRO users see zero ads

6. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for students preparing for the NIELIT O-Level examination. It is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Note that Google AdMob applies its own child-safety policies to the ads displayed.

7. Data Security

All data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's built-in security. We do not operate servers that hold your personal data. Connections to third-party services (AdMob, RevenueCat) are made over encrypted HTTPS.

8. Data Retention

  • Local app data (bookmarks, settings): retained until you clear app data or uninstall
  • Purchase records: retained by RevenueCat per their policy for transaction verification purposes
  • Ad data: managed entirely by Google per their data retention policies

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete local data — clear app data in Android Settings, or uninstall the app
  • Request purchase record deletion — contact us at itechnowizard12@gmail.com and we will work with RevenueCat to remove your records
  • Opt out of personalised advertising — via Android system settings as described above

See our Data Deletion page for full instructions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
