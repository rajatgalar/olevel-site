Data Deletion

How to delete your data from O-Level Prep

Good news: O-Level Prep does not require an account and stores almost no data about you. Most of your data is on your device only and is deleted the moment you uninstall the app.

What Data Exists and Where

Data Type Where Stored Deleted When
Bookmarks On your device App uninstalled or Clear App Data
Settings (theme, language, font size, notification time) On your device App uninstalled or Clear App Data
Quiz progress / session state On your device When you leave the quiz screen
Score card images On your device (cache) System clears cache automatically
PRO purchase record RevenueCat + Google Play Request required — see below
Advertising ID / ad interactions Google AdMob Reset via Android Settings — see below

Option 1 — Delete All Local Data (Instant)

This deletes your bookmarks, settings, and all on-device data immediately.

1
Open Android Settings Go to your phone's Settings app
2
Go to Apps → O-Level Prep Find the app in your app list
3
Tap "Storage" → "Clear Data" This deletes all local data including bookmarks and settings
4
Uninstall the app (optional) Uninstalling removes everything remaining on your device

Option 2 — Delete Purchase Records

If you purchased PRO, a purchase record exists with RevenueCat (to verify your entitlement) and with Google Play (as a transaction record). To request deletion:

Email us at itechnowizard12@gmail.com with subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the Google account email you used for the purchase. We will contact RevenueCat on your behalf to remove your records within 30 days.

Note: Google Play transaction records are subject to Google's own data retention policies and may not be fully removable on our end.
📧 Send Deletion Request

Option 3 — Reset Advertising Data

To reset or opt out of personalised advertising (Google AdMob):

1
Open Android Settings → Privacy → Ads (Location may vary by Android version and manufacturer)
2
Tap "Reset Advertising ID" This generates a new ID, breaking any link to your previous ad history
3
Tap "Opt out of Ads Personalisation" (optional) You will still see ads but they will not be personalised to you

Response Time

  • Local data — deleted instantly when you clear app data or uninstall
  • Purchase record deletion requests — processed within 30 days of your email
  • Ad ID reset — takes effect immediately via Android Settings
